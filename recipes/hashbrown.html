HashBrown Quiche

The Most Delicous Hashbrown Quiche you will ever taste

Looking for an easy hashbrown quiche to make for your family?

You are in the right place, as we are offering just that

Follow along

Ingredients

  • 4 cups shredded hash brown potatoes
  • 6 tablespoons butter, divided
  • seasoning salt or garlic powder to taste
  • 6 large eggs, divided
  • 1 cup diced cooked ham
  • 1/3 cup finely diced red bell pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).
  2. Place hash browns in a medium bowl. Melt 5 tablespoons butter and pour over hash browns
  3. Bake in preheated oven
  4. When crust is ready, spread ham mixture over the bottom
  5. Give it 10 More minutes and Voila !
