Alfredo Pasta

The Best Alfredo Pasta you will ever have

With this Alfredo Pasta Recipe you will

be able to impress all of your friends and family.

It is simply Irresistable

Ingredients

  • Pasta 500g
  • Cheese 200g
  • Chicken 300g
  • Milk 400ml
  • Parsley

Steps

  1. You Cut Up the chicken
  2. You cook the chicken in the pan
  3. Start boiling the pasta now
  4. Combine the pasta into the pan with chicken
  5. As they simmer together add the milk and slowly start adding the cheese
  6. Finish off with some Parsley and Enjoy !
